# My Clients - Nail Master Management System

## Overview

**My Clients** is a modern, developer-first platform designed specifically for nail masters and beauty professionals who need to efficiently manage their client base, services, and business operations. This comprehensive system consolidates appointment scheduling, service catalog management, pricing control, and real-time business analytics into a single, intuitive application.

The platform addresses a critical gap in the beauty industry by providing specialized tools that go beyond generic calendar applications, offering nail masters a dedicated solution for service-based businesses where pricing varies by service complexity, client retention matters, and performance metrics drive business growth.
