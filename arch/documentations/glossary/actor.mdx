## ðŸ‘¤ Salon Admin

**Definition:** The nail salon owner or business manager responsible for overall salon operations and staff management.

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Onboard and manage multiple nail masters on the platform
- Configure business settings, salon name, and contact information
- Monitor and approve service pricing across all nail masters
- View comprehensive business analytics and revenue reports
- Track performance metrics for each nail master
- Manage salon policies and appointment rules
- Access financial reporting and growth insights

**Access Level:** Full system access with administrative privileges

**Common Tasks:**
- Review monthly income dashboard for the entire salon
- Analyze which nail masters are most profitable
- Monitor client acquisition and retention trends
- Set salon-wide policies for cancellations and no-shows

---

## ðŸ‘¤ Nail Master

**Definition:** Independent service provider (nail technician) who manages their own schedule, clients, and services within the salon.

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Create and manage personal service offerings (gel nails, acrylics, nail art, etc.)
- Set and update individual service pricing
- Manage personal availability and block time for breaks
- Create and confirm appointments with clients
- Update appointment status (completed, cancelled, no-show)
- Build and maintain relationships with regular clients
- Track personal income and appointment statistics

**Access Level:** Limited access to own schedule, clients, services, and personal analytics

**Common Tasks:**
- View daily/weekly calendar and manage appointments
- Add new clients and maintain client notes
- Create service packages or special offers
- Track personal revenue and busiest time periods
- Receive appointment reminders and client confirmations

---

## ðŸ‘¤ Salon Client

**Definition:** End customer who books appointments for nail services and receives service from nail masters.

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Browse available nail services and pricing
- View appointment availability and book time slots
- Receive appointment confirmations and reminders
- Manage personal appointment history and preferences
- Update profile information and contact details

**Access Level:** Read-only access to service catalog and own appointment data; booking functionality only

**Common Tasks:**
- Search and view available appointment times
- Book appointments with preferred nail master
- Receive email/SMS reminders before appointments
- View past appointment history and service details
- Save preferred services and service preferences

## Actor Interactions

### Salon Admin
- **Primary Portal**: Staff Portal (Admin & Master Portal)
- **Key Responsibilities**: Business management, staff oversight, financial analytics, system configuration
- **Access Level**: Full system access including all modules and analytics

### Nail Master
- **Primary Portal**: Staff Portal (Admin & Master Portal)
- **Key Responsibilities**: Personal schedule management, client service delivery, appointment handling
- **Access Level**: Limited to own schedule, assigned clients, and service catalog

### Salon Client
- **Primary Portal**: Client Portal
- **Key Responsibilities**: Self-service appointment booking, profile management, service browsing
- **Access Level**: Personal appointments, history, and profile data only
