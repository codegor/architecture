# Key Concepts

## üìÖ Calendar Management

**Definition:** The core scheduling system in **My Clients** that enables nail masters to manage availability and appointments efficiently.

**Key Features:**
- **Multi-View Options** - Daily, weekly, and monthly calendar layouts
- **Availability Management** - Block time for breaks, lunch, or days off
- **Appointment Duration** - Auto-calculated based on selected service(s)
- **Conflict Prevention** - System prevents double-booking the same time slot
- **Real-Time Sync** - Changes visible immediately across all devices
- **Client Reminders** - Automatic email/SMS sent 24 hours and 1 hour before appointment

**Why It Matters:**
Eliminates scheduling chaos, reduces no-shows through reminders, and ensures nail masters never overbook themselves.

## üí∞ Dynamic Pricing

**Definition:** The ability for each nail master to set and adjust service prices based on their business model and market position.

**Pricing Capabilities:**
- **Individual Service Pricing** - Set unique prices for each service offered
- **Service Packages** - Create bundle deals with discounted combined pricing
- **Price Flexibility** - Update prices anytime based on demand, season, or complexity
- **Client-Specific Pricing** - Ability to offer special rates to loyal/regular clients
- **Complexity-Based Pricing** - Higher rates for intricate nail art or specialized services

**Business Applications:**
- Charge premium prices for complex designs or special requests
- Offer loyalty discounts to regular clients
- Create seasonal promotions (holiday specials, summer designs)
- Test pricing to find optimal revenue per service
- Track which price points generate most demand

## üìä Analytics & Reporting

**Definition:** Built-in business intelligence tools that help nail masters and salon owners understand their business performance.

**Key Analytics Available:**

**Monthly Income Dashboard**
- Total revenue for the period
- Revenue per service (which services make most money)
- Revenue per nail master (for salon owners)
- Comparison to previous months (growth trends)

**Client Statistics**
- Total number of clients served
- New clients acquired this month
- Repeat client rate and retention metrics
- Client acquisition cost analysis
- Most valuable client segments

**Service Performance**
- Revenue by service (ranked)
- Popularity metrics (most booked services)
- Average service price and duration
- Service demand trends
- Client preferences by service type

**Appointment Metrics**
- Total appointments booked and completed
- Cancellation rate and patterns
- No-show rate and frequency
- Booking lead time analysis
- Peak hours and busy periods

**Client Base Insights**
- Client segmentation (one-time vs. repeat)
- Lifetime value per client
- Service history and preferences
- Churn analysis (lost clients)
- Growth trajectory

## üë• Client Base Management

**Definition:** The system for storing, organizing, and leveraging information about all clients served.

**Managed Information:**
- **Contact Details** - Name, phone, email, preferred contact method
- **Service Preferences** - Favorite services, preferred nail master, style preferences
- **Service History** - Complete appointment history and services received
- **Special Requests** - Notes on allergies, nail sensitivities, color preferences
- **Frequency Tracking** - How often client books, average time between visits
- **Loyalty Status** - Identification of regular/repeat customers vs. one-time clients
- **Engagement Level** - Client responsiveness to reminders and communications

**Business Value:**
- Enable personalized service and client recognition
- Identify at-risk clients for targeted retention efforts
- Spot high-value clients deserving premium service
- Make informed recommendations based on history
- Build lasting client relationships

## üîî Notifications

**Definition:** Automated communication system that keeps clients informed and reduces no-shows.

**Notification Types:**
- **Booking Confirmation** - Sent immediately after client books appointment
- **24-Hour Reminder** - Proactive reminder one day before appointment
- **1-Hour Reminder** - Final reminder closer to appointment time
- **Cancellation Notice** - Notification when appointment is cancelled
- **Completion Thank You** - Post-appointment message and service feedback request
- **Promotional Messages** - Special offers or new service announcements

**Communication Channels:**
- **Email Notifications** - Detailed information and easy to reference
- **SMS Text Messages** - Immediate mobile notification for time-sensitive reminders
- **In-App Notifications** - Alerts within the client portal

**Impact:**
- Dramatically reduces no-show rate (critical for salon business)
- Improves client experience through professional communication
- Maintains client engagement between appointments
- Provides opportunities for upselling and feedback collection

## üîê Authentication & Authorization

**Definition:** Security system that controls who can access **My Clients** and what data they can see.

**Security Components:**
- **User Authentication** - Secure login with unique credentials
- **Role-Based Access Control** - Different permissions for Admin, Nail Master, Client
- **Session Management** - Secure session handling with timeout protection
- **Permission Enforcement** - Clients only see own data, Masters only see own clients
- **Data Isolation** - Strict separation between different users' data
- **Password Security** - Encrypted storage and secure password policies

**Access Control Rules:**
- **Salon Admin** - Full access to all salon data and all nail masters
- **Nail Master** - Access only to own schedule, own clients, own services
- **Salon Client** - Access only to own appointments and bookings
