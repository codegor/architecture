# Order Types

Order types represent the different ways nail services are offered and booked in the **My Clients** system.

## ðŸ’… Service

**Definition:** A single, individual nail service offering provided by a nail master.

**Characteristics:**
- Atomic service with fixed pricing set by the nail master
- Specific duration estimate (typically 15-120 minutes)
- Detailed service description and complexity level
- Service category (manicure, pedicure, nail art, repairs, treatments)
- Performance tracking (popularity, revenue contribution, client feedback)

**Real-World Examples:**
- **Gel Manicure** - 60 minutes - $35
- **Acrylic Full Set** - 90 minutes - $50
- **Nail Art Design** - 45 minutes - $20-40 (depending on complexity)
- **Acrylics Removal** - 30 minutes - $15
- **Dip Powder Manicure** - 45 minutes - $25
- **Gel Pedicure** - 75 minutes - $40
- **Quick Nail Repair** - 15 minutes - $10

**Nail Master Control:**
- Each nail master sets their own service prices
- Can update descriptions and duration estimates anytime
- Track which services generate most revenue
- Monitor client demand and booking frequency per service

## ðŸ“¦ Service Package

**Definition:** Bundled group of complementary services offered together at a special combined price.

**Characteristics:**
- Contains 2+ related services
- Combined pricing (typically 10-20% discount vs. individual services)
- Fixed total duration or flexible duration
- Marketing tool for upselling and increasing average transaction value
- Client incentive for booking multiple services at once

**Real-World Examples:**
- **Bridal Glow Package** - Gel Manicure + Gel Pedicure + Nail Art - $80 (save $15)
- **Full Beauty Refresh** - Gel Manicure + Gel Pedicure + Acrylics - $110
- **Weekend Ready** - Gel Manicure + Nail Design - $50
- **Maintenance Bundle** - 4 Gel Manicure appointments (booked monthly) - $120/month
- **Spa Luxury** - Gel Manicure + Pedicure + Hand Massage - $75

**Business Benefits:**
- Increases average client spending
- Encourages clients to try multiple services
- Creates perceived value through bundled discounts
- Simplifies pricing presentation for complex service combinations

## ðŸ“… Appointment

**Definition:** A scheduled booking of one or more services with a specific client at a specific date and time.

**Characteristics:**
- Links selected service(s) to a client and nail master
- Specific date, start time, and calculated duration
- Status tracking throughout the appointment lifecycle
- Client communication points (confirmation, reminders, completion)
- Price locked in at booking time
- Optional client notes (special requests, nail preferences, allergies)

**Status Lifecycle:**
- **Scheduled** - Confirmed appointment awaiting service delivery
- **Completed** - Service successfully delivered and payment processed
- **Cancelled** - Appointment cancelled by client or nail master (may incur fee)
- **No-Show** - Client failed to arrive at scheduled time without cancellation

**Related Information:**
- Client name, phone, email
- Selected service(s) or package
- Nail master assigned to appointment
- Appointment duration (auto-calculated from services)
- Total price at time of booking
- Special client notes or preferences (e.g., "prefers pink tones", "sensitive nails")
- Payment status and method

**Communication Triggers:**
- Confirmation email/SMS upon booking
- 24-hour reminder notification
- 1-hour reminder notification
- Completion confirmation and thank you message

## ðŸ“… Subscription Model

**Definition:** A recurring service model where clients pay a fixed fee for a set number of services over a specified period.

**Characteristics:**
- Fixed monthly fee for a defined number of services (e.g., 2 services per month)
- Encourages regular client visits and loyalty
- Simplifies budgeting for clients
- Can include rollover services if unused within the month
- Offers exclusive discounts on additional services

**Real-World Examples:**
- **Monthly Nail Care Subscription** - 2 services per month for $60 (save $10)
- **Quarterly Beauty Package** - 6 services over 3 months for $150 (save $30)

**Business Benefits:**
- Predictable revenue stream for nail masters
- Increases client retention and satisfaction
- Encourages clients to explore additional services
